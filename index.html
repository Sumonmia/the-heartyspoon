FAQ Section

What are your restaurant's hours of operation?

We are open Monday to Saturday from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM. On Sundays, we are open from 12:00 PM to 9:00 PM.

Do you offer delivery or takeout?

Yes, we offer both delivery and takeout services. You can place your order through our website or through our delivery partners, such as Foodpanda and Pathao.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please let our staff know about any dietary restrictions when ordering.
